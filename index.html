Are you a woman in STEM?

Join Our Community!

Welcome to Women In STEM at UTSA! We're a vibrant community empowering women in STEM. Our mission is to foster belonging and camaraderie, providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for academic and professional growth. Whether you're exploring interests or a seasoned STEM veteran, find a welcoming environment here. Through networking events, workshops, speakers, and mentorship, we create connections and empower each other. Join us to break down barriers, shatter stereotypes, and build a brighter future for women in STEM.
Below are some requirements to become an active member.

Join Us Now!

Attend 3/5 General Meetings

Attend at least 3 general meetings this semester!

Attend 4 WIS Socials

Come have fun and meet with other women in STEM by attending at least 4 of our socials!

Attend 2 Volunteer Events

Reach out to the community once with WIS and once with a cause of your choice.

Participate in at least 2 WIS Fundraisers

Help us raise money for our organization at least 2 times this semester!

Attend 2 Professional Development events held by WIS

Come network and gain professional skills with other WIS.

Pay Dues

Pay dues with the WIS treasurer! Dues are $15 for 1 semester and $25 for 2 semesters!
